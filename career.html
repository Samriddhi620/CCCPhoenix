Telecommuting Jobs



Freelance Writer

If you are already a skilled communicator, doing freelance writing projects can be a great way to earn money. Publishers, organizations, and even various types of individual professionals are looking for writers for a variety of temporary projects, such as marketing materials, grant proposals, articles, blog posts, advertising copy, website content, white papers, and books. Also, writing is something you can do at home.

Median hourly pay: $24.07



Sales Representative

Speaking of part-time jobs for pregnant women, selling products or services is still a good choice. After all, this field usually offers flexible working hours, so you can work as much as you need, and you can even work full-time if you want to earn the highest commission. Effective sales techniques can usually be learned quickly, especially if you find an experienced mentor.

Median hourly pay (selling wholesale products): $28.81

Median hourly pay (selling services): $26.99

Private Tutor

Did you excel in any academic subjects when you were in school? Are you an expert in a certain area? Many students benefit from extra-curricular personalized tutoring in subjects that they have difficulties. In addition, nowadays, many tutors provide online services and conduct conversations with customers through video chat technologies such as Skype and Facetime.

Median hourly pay: $24.65
